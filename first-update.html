Update the school list function.

Introduction

According to my observation, students tend to have trouble coming up their school list. Thus, integrating a function that can help students to choose their school according to their intended major, gpa, sat, toefl, activity, and other accomplishment would be a good idea.

How it works

Students need to input their highest sat score, their highest toefl score, and their averaged gpa. Then, they need to choose the activity they have done from a range of activity and the level of those activities. Finally, they would need to choose the accomplishment they gained during their high school year. The School List Function would generate a result, a list of school, back to the students once they finish uploading their information.

The list of school would just be a reference for the students to improve their choices.

When will the function be published

The function would be published together with the college info function.
